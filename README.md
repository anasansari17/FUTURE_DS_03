# Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

## 📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes an e-commerce dataset to understand user behavior across the marketing funnel. The objective is to identify where customers drop off in the journey from product discovery to final purchase.

The dashboard visualizes key funnel metrics and highlights high-performing brands and product categories that drive revenue.

The analysis helps businesses optimize their marketing strategies and improve conversion rates.



## 🎯 Problem Statement

E-commerce platforms often face challenges converting website visitors into paying customers. Understanding how users move through the marketing funnel is essential for improving customer acquisition and revenue.

This project answers the following questions:

* How many users move from product view to purchase?
* Where do most users drop off in the funnel?
* Which brands generate the most purchases?
* Which product categories drive the most revenue?


## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

* **Microsoft Power BI** – Dashboard creation and data visualization
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Calculating funnel metrics
* **Data Cleaning** – Handling missing values and formatting timestamps
* **Data Visualization** – Creating interactive charts and filters


## 📊 Dashboard Components

The Power BI dashboard includes several analytical visualizations:

**1️⃣ KPI Cards**

* Total product views
* Total add-to-cart events
* Total purchases
* Overall conversion rate

**2️⃣ Marketing Funnel Visualization**
Shows the customer journey:
View → Cart → Purchase

**3️⃣ Brand Performance**
Identifies brands generating the highest number of purchases.

**4️⃣ Traffic vs Conversion**
Compares product views with purchases across brands.

**5️⃣ Revenue by Category**
Highlights the product categories contributing the most revenue.

**6️⃣ Interactive Filters**
Users can filter the dashboard by:

* Brand
* Product Category
* Price Range

---

## 📉 Key Insights

1. The largest drop-off occurs between **product views and cart additions**, indicating that many users browse products but do not proceed to add them to the cart.

2. **Samsung and Apple generate the highest number of purchases**, showing strong customer demand for these brands.

3. Electronics categories contribute the **largest share of revenue**, highlighting their importance in overall sales performance.

4. Some purchases occur without recorded cart events, suggesting direct purchase behavior or tracking differences.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following improvements could increase conversions:

* Improve product page design to encourage cart additions
* Highlight high-performing brands like Samsung and Apple in marketing campaigns
* Offer promotional incentives to convert product views into purchases
* Optimize product categories generating the highest revenue



## 🚀 Project Outcome

This project demonstrates how marketing funnel analysis can help businesses understand user behavior and optimize conversion performance. By identifying bottlenecks in the funnel and analyzing product performance, companies can improve marketing strategies and increase revenue.
